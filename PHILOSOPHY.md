# Philosophy

Built for everyone who needs it to work.

No install. No account. No subscription. No internet required.
One file. Runs on old hardware, borrowed computers,
library terminals, USB sticks passed between friends.

Digital simplicity with access in mind in the goal. 
If you have every resource available and use this anyway — welcome.
If you're on a borrowed computer with twenty minutes on the clock — welcome.
The utility does not know the difference.
 
Take what you need, and compost or leave the rest.
Godspeed.
